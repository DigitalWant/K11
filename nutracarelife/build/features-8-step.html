NutraCare Difference

Milk Source 8 Point Protection Plan

We pride ourselves on giving NutraCare babies the best start in life. That means having a stringent operating system that guarantees quality assurance from the dairy to your door. Here’s how our 8 Point Protection Plan works to safeguard your infant formula:

STEP1

Experienced Dairy Farmers Raise Happy Cows For The Best Quality Milk

It all starts with the cows that graze in the heart of Australia’s most famous dairy region – Gippsland. Here, the sky is blue, the grass is green and the cows roam free. Raising cows that are happy and healthy is the farmer’s number one priority. These lucky cows are raised on natural pastures and fed only the best nutrition, so they produce milk that’s pure, fresh and of the highest quality.

STEP2

Milk Powder Production

Milk drying, blending and packing is a serious art. Burra Foods Factory, Australia’s newest multi-million dollar milk dryer facility, uses the world’s most advanced technology to produce our high-end dry milk powder.

Qualified dairy technicians then blend formulations with essential vitamins and minerals to ensure the right balance of nutrients goes into your baby’s formula. These are wet blended to ensure optimal consistency and no lumps. (Dry blending is an inferior process where all ingredients are mixed in a dry powder format).

At the ViPlus Dairy factory, 120 years of experience of dairy production combines with state-of-the-art machinery installed in 2014 to ensure the highest standard of hygiene is met. We guarantee that all ingredients in NutraCare products comply with Australian diary and manufacturing standards, including HACCP, and meet China’s stringent GB standards.

STEP3

Trucking and Transport

Our drivers collect the NutraCare containers with your individually manufactured personal supply from the ViPlus Dairy Factory in Toora. They drive carefully non-stop 200kms to Melbourne’s Wharf, where the goods are delivered into the hands of Steve Duckworth’s team at Hellmann International, ready for the next part of their important journey.

STEP4

Factory Quality Control

Our factory quality audits are handpicked based on lists of stringent criteria and rigorous processes, high responsiveness and a say-no attitude to imperfection. They don’t just do the job but love it with a sense of corporate and civic responsibility, to ensure safe infant food. Before your order leaves the factory, every batch is tested by Dairy Technical Services – Australia’s independent dairy specialist testing laboratory – before it can receive the final health certificate from the Australian Government.

STEP5

Shipping

The head of domestic delivery transfers responsibility for the goods to Steve Duckworth and his team at Hellmann International, a world-class global freight and logistics company. Steve is Senior Manager and an acknowledged expert in the industry, trusted and admired for his experience and integrity. Hellmann complies with international shipping standards and even surpasses the certified requirements. Our partnership with Hellmann ensures your order and personal production is safeguarded at every step of the way.

STEP6

Customs

When the goods reach China they go through the rigorous check of CIQ (China Inspection and Quarantine) to ensure again that the product complies with the strictest China GB Standards. Manufacturers of dairy products, particularly infant formula, are subject to even stricter accreditation for the registration process than other food and beverage exporters. More than 32 tests are conducted.

STEP7

Warehouse

Following its release from China CIQ testing, your order is collected and taken directly to Hellmann’s specialist warehouse facilities. With special Chinese licensees for handling of infant formula, Hellmann China is responsible for taking delivery of your NutraCare product, picking your individual shipment, keeping it safe, packing it and addressing it. We proudly introduce Julian Base, who head’s up Hellmann’s NutraCare business in China, and manages NutraCare’s end-to-end logistic network across China.

STEP8

Delivery

NutraCare provides members with the most efficient and professional door-to-door service, which is why we choose SF Express for the last step in the delivery process. SF Express is China’s must trusted logistic company and its systems have been integrated with NutraCare customer service team in China so that we can keep track of your delivery and efficiently handle any questions you have.
